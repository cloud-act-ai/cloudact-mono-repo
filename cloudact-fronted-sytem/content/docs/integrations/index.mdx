Connect your providers to start tracking costs and usage.

## Supported Providers

| Provider | Type | What You'll See |
|----------|------|-----------------|
| **OpenAI** | AI | GPT-4, GPT-3.5 usage and costs |
| **Anthropic** | AI | Claude usage and costs |
| **DeepSeek** | AI | DeepSeek usage and costs |
| **Google Cloud** | Cloud | Compute, storage, and service costs |

## How to Connect

1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Integrations**
2. Click on the provider you want to connect
3. Follow the setup instructions
4. Enter your credentials
5. Click **Save**

Your data will appear on your dashboard within a few minutes.

## Security

Your credentials are:

- **Encrypted** before storage
- **Never visible** after you save them
- **Only used** to fetch your usage data

<Callout type="info">
  We recommend creating dedicated API keys with read-only access for CloudAct.
</Callout>
