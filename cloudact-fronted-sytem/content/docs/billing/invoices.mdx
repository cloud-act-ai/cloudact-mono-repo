---
title: Invoices & Receipts
description: View and download your billing history
---

Access all your invoices and download receipts for your records.

## View Your Invoices

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Billing**
3. Select **Invoice History**

## Invoice Information

Each invoice shows:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Date** | When you were billed |
| **Amount** | Total charged |
| **Status** | Paid, Pending, or Failed |
| **Download** | PDF receipt link |

## Download a Receipt

1. Find the invoice in your billing history
2. Click the **Download** or **PDF** link
3. Save the file for your records

## Invoice Statuses

| Status | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Paid** | Payment successful |
| **Pending** | Payment processing |
| **Failed** | Payment declined |

## Failed Payments

If your payment fails:

1. You'll receive an email notification
2. We'll retry the payment automatically
3. Update your payment method if needed

<Callout type="warning">
  After multiple failed payments, your account access may be paused. Update your payment method to restore access.
</Callout>

## Update Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Billing**
3. Click **Update Payment Method**
4. Enter your new card details
5. Click **Save**

## Tax & Business Information

To update your billing address or add a VAT/Tax ID:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Billing**
3. Update your billing information
4. Click **Save**

Future invoices will include your updated information.
