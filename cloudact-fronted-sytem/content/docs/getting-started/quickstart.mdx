Already familiar with the basics? Here's the fastest way to get started.

## 1. Sign Up

Visit [cloudact.ai/signup](/signup) and create your account with your work email.

## 2. Select a Plan

Choose **Starter** if you're just exploring, or **Professional** for full features.

## 3. Add an Integration

After logging in:
- Click **Settings** in the sidebar
- Click **Integrations**
- Select your provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
- Paste your API key and click **Save**

## 4. View Your Dashboard

Go to **Dashboard** to see:
- Real-time cost tracking
- Usage breakdowns by model
- Spending trends over time

## You're All Set!

Your costs will sync automatically. Check back in a few minutes to see your data.
