This guide provides detailed instructions for creating and configuring your CloudAct account.

## Before You Begin

Before creating your account, have the following ready:

- **Work email address** - This will be your login credential
- **Phone number** - For account verification and security
- **Company information** - Name, type, and preferred settings

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Navigate to Signup

Go to [cloudact.ai/signup](/signup) to begin the registration process.

![CloudAct Signup Page](/docs/images/signup-step1.png)

### Step 2: Enter Personal Information

Fill in the **Account** section (Step 1 of 2):

#### First Name and Last Name

Enter your full name as it should appear in your profile:

```
First Name: John
Last Name: Smith
```

<Callout type="info">
  Your name will be visible to team members and in notifications.
</Callout>

#### Email Address

Enter your work email address:

```
Email: john.smith@acme.com
```

- Use your company email for business accounts
- This email will receive important notifications
- You'll use this email to log in

#### Password

Create a strong password:

```
Password: ••••••••••••
```

**Password requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- Mix of letters, numbers, and symbols recommended
- Avoid common passwords

#### Phone Number

Select your country code and enter your phone number:

```
Country: +1 (US/Canada)
Phone: 555-123-4567
```

<Callout type="tip">
  **Auto-formatting**: Your phone number is automatically formatted as you type based on your selected country code!
</Callout>

### Step 3: Set Up Your Organization

Fill in the **Organization** section (Step 2 of 2):

#### Company Name

Enter your company or organization name:

```
Company Name: Acme Inc.
```

- 2-100 characters allowed
- Used as your workspace name
- Can be changed later in settings

#### Company Type

Select the type that best describes your organization:

| Type | Best For |
|------|----------|
| **Personal** | Individual developers, freelancers |
| **Startup** | Early-stage companies |
| **Agency** | Service providers, consultancies |
| **Company** | Established businesses |
| **Educational** | Schools, universities, bootcamps |

#### Currency

Select your default currency for displaying costs:

**Supported currencies:**
- USD ($) - US Dollar
- EUR (€) - Euro
- GBP (£) - British Pound
- JPY (JP¥) - Japanese Yen
- CHF (Fr) - Swiss Franc
- CAD (C$) - Canadian Dollar
- AUD (A$) - Australian Dollar
- CNY (CN¥) - Chinese Yuan
- INR (₹) - Indian Rupee
- SGD (S$) - Singapore Dollar
- AED (د.إ) - UAE Dirham

- QAR (ر.ق) - Qatari Riyal

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"Invalid phone number" error**
- Ensure you've selected the correct country code
- Enter only the local number (without country code)
- Check the number follows your country's format

**"Email already registered"**
- Use the [login page](/login) if you already have an account
- Try the "Forgot Password" link to recover access

**Form not submitting**
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check password meets minimum requirements
- Verify email format is correct
