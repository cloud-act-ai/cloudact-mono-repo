Welcome to CloudAct! This guide will walk you through setting up your account and viewing your first cost report.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [cloudact.ai/signup](/signup)
2. Enter your **email address** and create a **password**
3. Fill in your **company name** and select your **industry**
4. Click **Create Account**

You'll receive a confirmation email. Click the link to verify your account.

## Step 2: Choose Your Plan

After verifying your email, you'll be asked to select a plan:

| Plan | Best For | Team Size |
|------|----------|-----------|
| **Starter** | Small teams getting started | Up to 2 members |
| **Professional** | Growing teams with multiple providers | Up to 10 members |
| **Enterprise** | Large organizations | Unlimited |

Select your plan and complete the payment process.

## Step 3: Connect Your First Integration

Once your account is active:

1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Integrations**
2. Click on the provider you want to connect (e.g., OpenAI)
3. Enter your API key
4. Click **Save**

That's it! Your costs will start appearing on your dashboard within a few minutes.

## Step 4: Explore Your Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:

- **Total Spend** - Your current billing period costs
- **Cost Trends** - How your spending changes over time
- **Top Providers** - Which services cost the most
