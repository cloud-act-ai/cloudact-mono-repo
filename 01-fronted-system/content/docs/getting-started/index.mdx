Welcome to CloudAct! This guide will walk you through setting up your account and viewing your first cost report.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Creating your CloudAct account is a simple 2-step process.

### Step 1a: Personal Information

Visit [cloudact.ai/signup](/signup) and enter your personal details:

![Signup Step 1 - Account Information](/docs/images/signup-step1.png)

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **First Name** | Your first/given name | John |
| **Last Name** | Your last/family name | Smith |
| **Email Address** | Your work email (used for login) | john.smith@acme.com |
| **Password** | Minimum 8 characters | SecurePass123! |
| **Phone Number** | Your contact number with country code | +1 555-123-4567 |

<Callout type="info">
  **Phone Number Formatting**: Your phone number is automatically formatted based on your selected country code. For US (+1), the format is XXX-XXX-XXXX. Different countries have different formats.
</Callout>

Click **Continue** to proceed to organization setup.

### Step 1b: Organization Setup

Configure your workspace settings:

| Field | Description | Options |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Company Name** | Your organization name | Acme Inc. |
| **Company Type** | Type of organization | Personal, Startup, Agency, Company, Educational |
| **Currency** | Default currency for cost display | USD, EUR, GBP, INR, and 12 more |
| **Timezone** | Your local timezone | 15 major timezones supported |

Click **Create Account** to complete registration.

<Callout type="tip">
  Your organization settings can be changed later from **Settings** â†’ **Organization**.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Choose Your Plan

After creating your account, you'll be directed to select a subscription plan:

| Plan | Best For | Team Size | Monthly Price |
|------|----------|-----------|---------------|
| **Starter** | Small teams getting started | Up to 2 members | $19/month |
| **Professional** | Growing teams with multiple providers | Up to 10 members | $69/month |
| **Scale** | Large organizations | Up to 50 members | $199/month |

<Callout type="info">
  All plans include a **14-day free trial**. No credit card required to start.
</Callout>

Select your plan and complete the payment process through our secure Stripe checkout.

## Step 3: Connect Your First Integration

Once your account is active:

1. Go to **Integrations** in the sidebar
2. Click on the provider you want to connect:
   - **GenAI**: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, Cursor
   - **Cloud**: Google Cloud, AWS, Azure
   - **SaaS**: Slack, GitHub, Figma, Notion
3. Enter your API key or connect via OAuth
4. Click **Save**

<Callout type="success">
  Your costs will start appearing on your dashboard within a few minutes!
</Callout>

## Step 4: Explore Your Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:

- **Total Spend** - Your current billing period costs across all providers
- **Cost Trends** - How your spending changes over time with interactive charts
- **Top Providers** - Which services cost the most
- **Usage Breakdown** - Detailed breakdown by model, service, or team
