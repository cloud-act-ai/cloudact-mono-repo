Your profile contains your personal information, security settings, and account management options.

## Accessing Profile Settings

Click on your **name/avatar** in the sidebar footer to access your Personal Settings page.

![Personal Settings - Profile Tab](/docs/images/profile-personal-info.png)

## Profile Tab

The Profile tab contains your personal information.

### Personal Information

| Field | Description | Editable |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Email Address** | Your login email (read-only) | No - Contact support |
| **First Name** | Your first name | Yes |
| **Last Name** | Your last name | Yes |
| **Phone Number** | Contact number with country code | Yes |
| **Timezone** | Your personal timezone preference | Yes |

<Callout type="info">
  Your email address is set during account creation. Contact support to update your email.
</Callout>

### Phone Number Format

Select your country code and enter your local number:

| Country | Code | Format |
|---------|------|--------|
| US/Canada | +1 | 555-123-4567 |
| UK | +44 | 7911-123-4567 |
| India | +91 | 98765-43210 |
| Australia | +61 | 412-345-678 |

### Saving Changes

After updating your information, click **Save Changes** to apply your updates.

## Security Tab

The Security tab manages your authentication settings.

![Personal Settings - Security Tab](/docs/images/profile-security.png)

### Password Management

Reset your password via email verification:

1. Click **Reset Password**
2. Check your email for a reset link
3. Follow the link to create a new password

### Security Tips

- Use a strong, unique password for your account
- Never share your password or reset links with anyone
- Log out when using shared or public devices

<Callout type="tip">
  Enable browser password manager integration for secure, convenient access.
</Callout>

## Danger Zone

The Danger Zone tab contains account deletion options.

![Personal Settings - Danger Zone](/docs/images/profile-danger-zone.png)

### Organizations You Own

Before deleting your account, you must handle organizations you own:

| Action | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| **Transfer Ownership** | When you want the organization to continue |
| **Delete Organization** | When the organization is no longer needed |

### Delete Account

<Callout type="error">
  **Warning:** Deleting your account permanently removes all your personal data and access. This action cannot be undone.
</Callout>

**Requirements before deletion:**
1. Transfer ownership or delete all organizations you own
2. Ensure you have no pending billing issues

**To delete your account:**
1. Handle all owned organizations first
2. The **Delete Account** button will become enabled
3. Click **Delete Account** and confirm

## Notification Preferences

Control which emails you receive:

| Notification | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Cost Alerts** | When spending exceeds your thresholds |
| **Weekly Summary** | Weekly cost overview email |
| **Team Updates** | When team members join or leave |
| **Product Updates** | New features and improvements |

Toggle each notification on or off based on your preferences.

## Best Practices

1. **Keep contact info current** - Ensures you receive important notifications
2. **Use a strong password** - Protect your account with unique credentials
3. **Review security settings regularly** - Maintain account security
4. **Set correct timezone** - Ensures accurate time displays in your view
