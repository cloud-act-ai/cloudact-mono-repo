Personal Settings let you manage your profile, security, and account preferences.

## Accessing Personal Settings

Click your **name/avatar** in the sidebar footer to access Personal Settings.

## Quick Overview

| Section | What You Can Do |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Profile** | Edit name, phone, timezone, view email |
| **Security** | Reset password, view security tips |
| **Danger Zone** | Delete your account |

## Personal vs Organization Settings

| Setting Type | Location | Scope |
|--------------|----------|-------|
| **Personal** | Personal Settings | Only affects you |
| **Organization** | Org Settings | Affects all members |

### Personal Settings

- Your name and contact info
- Your password
- Your timezone preference
- Your account deletion

### Organization Settings

- Organization name and branding
- Team members
- Billing and subscription
- Integrations
- Hierarchy structure

<Callout type="info">
  Your personal timezone affects how dates appear in your view. The organization timezone affects pipeline schedules and reports.
</Callout>
