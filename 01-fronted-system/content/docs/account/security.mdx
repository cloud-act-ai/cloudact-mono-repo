Keep your account secure by managing your password and security settings.

## Change Your Password

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Confirm your new password
6. Click **Update Password**

<Callout type="info">
  Choose a strong password with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
</Callout>

## Forgot Your Password?

If you can't remember your password:

1. Go to the [login page](/login)
2. Click **Forgot password?**
3. Enter your email address
4. Click **Send Reset Link**
5. Check your email for the reset link
6. Click the link and enter your new password

<Callout type="warning">
  Reset links expire after 24 hours. Request a new one if yours has expired.
</Callout>

## Sign Out

To sign out of your account:

1. Click your **profile icon** in the top-right corner
2. Click **Sign Out**

You'll be signed out of all devices.

## Delete Your Account

<Callout type="error">
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
</Callout>

To delete your account:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Danger Zone**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Enter your password to confirm
4. Click **Permanently Delete**

All your data will be removed within 30 days.
