Collaborate with your team by inviting members to your organization.

## Invite Team Members

1. Go to **Settings** → **Members**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role:
   - **Admin** - Full access to all settings
   - **Collaborator** - Can view and manage integrations
   - **Read Only** - Can only view dashboards and reports
5. Click **Send Invite**

They'll receive an email with instructions to join your organization.

## Manage Existing Members

### Change a Member's Role

1. Go to **Settings** → **Members**
2. Find the member in the list
3. Click the role dropdown next to their name
4. Select the new role

### Remove a Member

1. Go to **Settings** → **Members**
2. Find the member in the list
3. Click the **...** menu
4. Click **Remove Member**
5. Confirm the removal

<Callout type="info">
  Removed members lose access immediately but can be re-invited later.
</Callout>

## Team Limits

Your plan determines how many team members you can have:

| Plan | Team Members |
|------|--------------|
| Starter | 2 |
| Professional | 10 |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

Need more seats? [Upgrade your plan](/user-docs/billing/plans).

## Pending Invites

View and manage pending invites:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Members**
2. Click the **Pending** tab
3. You can **Resend** or **Cancel** any invite
