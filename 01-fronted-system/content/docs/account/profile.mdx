Your profile contains your personal information and display preferences.

## Update Your Profile

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Update any of the following:
   - **Display Name** - How your name appears to team members
   - **Email Address** - Your login email (requires verification)
   - **Profile Photo** - Upload a picture or use your initials

3. Click **Save Changes**

## Change Your Email

To change your email address:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Click **Change Email**
3. Enter your new email address
4. Check your inbox for a verification link
5. Click the link to confirm the change

<Callout type="warning">
  You'll need to use your new email to sign in after changing it.
</Callout>

## Notification Preferences

Control which emails you receive:

| Notification | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Cost Alerts** | When spending exceeds your thresholds |
| **Weekly Summary** | Weekly cost overview email |
| **Team Updates** | When team members join or leave |
| **Product Updates** | New features and improvements |

Toggle each notification on or off based on your preferences.
