Configure your organization's settings, team structure, and billing from the Org Settings menu.

## Accessing Org Settings

Click **Org Settings** in the sidebar to expand the settings menu.

## Quick Overview

| Section | What You Can Do |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Organization** | Edit org name, logo, locale settings, backend connection |
| **Hierarchy** | Manage departments, projects, and teams for cost allocation |
| **Usage & Quotas** | View API usage and plan limits |
| **Team Members** | Invite users, manage roles, remove members |
| **Billing** | View subscription, manage payment, download invoices |

## Role-Based Access

Not all settings are available to all users:

| Setting | Owner | Admin | Member |
|---------|-------|-------|--------|
| Organization | Full | View | View |
| Hierarchy | Full | Full | View |
| Usage & Quotas | Full | Full | View |
| Team Members | Full | Invite/Remove | View |
| Billing | Full | View | - |

<Callout type="info">
  Only organization owners can access billing and danger zone settings.
</Callout>
