## Roles and Permissions

### Role Types

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access, can delete organization |
| **Admin** | Full access except billing and danger zone |
| **Member** | View-only access to dashboards |

### Permission Matrix

| Feature | Owner | Admin | Member |
|---------|-------|-------|--------|
| View dashboards | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| View cost analytics | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Run pipelines | Yes | Yes | No |
| Manage integrations | Yes | Yes | No |
| Invite members | Yes | Yes | No |
| Remove members | Yes | Yes | No |
| Change roles | Yes | Yes | No |
| Edit org settings | Yes | No | No |
| Manage billing | Yes | No | No |
| Delete organization | Yes | No | No |

## Inviting Team Members

### Sending Invites

1. Click **Invite Member**
2. Enter their email address
3. Select their role (Admin or Member)
4. Click **Send Invite**

<Callout type="info">
  Invitees receive an email with a link to join your organization.
</Callout>

### Invite Limits

Based on your plan:

| Plan | Member Limit |
|------|--------------|
| Starter | 2 members |
| Professional | 10 members |
| Scale | 50 members |

### Pending Invites

View and manage pending invitations:

- **Resend** - Send reminder email
- **Revoke** - Cancel the invitation

## Managing Members

### Changing Roles

1. Click the role dropdown next to a member
2. Select new role
3. Confirm change

<Callout type="warning">
  You cannot change the owner's role. Transfer ownership first.
</Callout>

### Removing Members

1. Click the remove icon next to a member
2. Confirm removal
3. Member loses access immediately

### Transferring Ownership

To transfer ownership:

1. Ensure new owner is already a member
2. Go to member list
3. Click **Transfer Ownership** next to the member
4. Confirm transfer

<Callout type="error">
  Ownership transfer is permanent. The previous owner becomes an Admin.
</Callout>

## Seat Management

### Understanding Seats

- Each member occupies one seat
- Pending invites count as occupied seats
- Removing members frees seats

### Seat Usage

| Status | Counts as Seat |
|--------|----------------|
| Active member | Yes |
| Pending invite | Yes |
| Revoked invite | No |
| Removed member | No |

## Best Practices

1. **Use appropriate roles** - Give minimum necessary access
2. **Regular audits** - Review members quarterly
3. **Remove promptly** - Remove departing employees immediately
4. **Multiple admins** - Have at least 2 admins for redundancy

## Troubleshooting

### Invite Not Received

- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address spelling
- Resend invitation
- Ask recipient to add cloudact.ai to contacts

### Cannot Remove Member

- Cannot remove yourself
- Cannot remove the owner (transfer ownership first)
- Check your role permissions
