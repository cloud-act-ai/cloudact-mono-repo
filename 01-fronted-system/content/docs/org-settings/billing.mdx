## Changing Plans

### Upgrading

1. Click **Change Plan**
2. Select new plan
3. Review prorated charges
4. Confirm upgrade
5. New limits apply immediately

### Downgrading

1. Click **Change Plan**
2. Select lower plan
3. Review what you'll lose
4. Confirm downgrade
5. Takes effect at next billing cycle

<Callout type="warning">
  Before downgrading, ensure you're within the new plan's limits (members, integrations).
</Callout>

## Payment Methods

### Adding a Card

1. Click **Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Card**
3. Enter card details
4. Click **Save**

### Updating Card

1. Click **Payment Methods**
2. Click **Update** on existing card
3. Enter new details
4. Click **Save**

### Removing Card

1. Add a new card first
2. Set new card as default
3. Remove old card

## Invoices

### Viewing Invoices

See all past invoices:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Date** | Invoice date |
| **Amount** | Total charged |
| **Status** | Paid, Pending, Failed |
| **Download** | PDF link |

### Downloading Invoices

1. Find invoice in list
2. Click **Download PDF**
3. Invoice downloads to your device

### Invoice Details

Each invoice includes:
- Invoice number
- Billing period
- Plan details
- Tax (if applicable)
- Payment method used

## Billing Portal

Access full billing management:

1. Click **Manage Billing**
2. Opens Stripe billing portal
3. Update payment methods
4. View full invoice history
5. Update billing address

## Trial Period

New accounts include a free trial:

- **Duration**: 14 days
- **Features**: Full access to selected plan
- **Payment**: Required at trial end

### Trial Ending

You'll receive notifications:
- 7 days before trial ends
- 3 days before trial ends
- On trial end date

## Failed Payments

### What Happens

1. **First failure**: Retry after 24 hours
2. **Second failure**: Retry after 3 days
3. **Third failure**: Account marked past due

### Past Due Account

Features limited until payment resolved:
- Dashboard view-only
- Cannot add integrations
- Pipeline runs paused

### Resolving Payment Issues

1. Update payment method
2. Retry payment
3. Contact support if issues persist

## Canceling Subscription

<Callout type="error">
  Cancellation takes effect at the end of your billing period. You retain access until then.
</Callout>

To cancel:

1. Click **Cancel Subscription**
2. Select cancellation reason
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Access continues until period end

### After Cancellation

- Data retained for 30 days
- Can reactivate anytime
- Export data before deletion
