Welcome to the CloudAct.ai user guide. This documentation will help you get the most out of our platform.

## What is CloudAct?

CloudAct.ai helps you track and optimize your AI and cloud spending. With CloudAct, you can:

- **Monitor costs** across OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, and Google Cloud
- **View usage trends** with easy-to-read charts and dashboards
- **Manage your team** with role-based permissions
- **Set up alerts** when spending exceeds thresholds
